About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a B.S. in Nutritional Sciences. After making the decision to not pursue medical school, I decided I wanted to take my career in a different direction.

I would describe myself as being outgoing, enthusiastic, and passionate about things I find interest in. I am focused and always trying to learn. I try to look at difficult situations as opportunities to grow rather than something to be afraid of. I try my best to make decisions in life that reflect the qualities of independence, bravery, and courage. I enjoy experiences that provide me with a strong feeling of discovery and satisfaction.

During my free time I like to watch netflix and learn new recipes. As you might not know, I chose to major in nutritional sciences because I wanted to know more about the biochemistry of food. Being healthy and working out are also a big part of my life–as I could not see myself without them.
